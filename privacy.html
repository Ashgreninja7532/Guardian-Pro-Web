Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Guardian Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.


2. Data Collection

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All processing happens locally on your device.


3. Permissions Usage

  • Usage Statistics: Used solely to detect when a locked app is opened. No usage history is stored.
  • Camera: Used only for the "Intruder Selfie" feature. Photos are saved locally to your device gallery. We do not have access to them.
  • Overlay: Used to display the lock screen over other apps.
  • Device Admin: Used optionally to prevent unauthorized uninstallation of the app.

4. Biometric Data

We use the standard Android Biometric API. We do not have access to your raw fingerprint or face data; we only receive a "Success/Fail" signal from the Android System.


5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:
